About Us

Established in 1965, MicroMetl is currently the largest independent manufacturer of accessories for commercial heating and cooling equipment. We Offer A Full Line of Accessories.

Roof curbs, specialty curbs, concentric duct packages, outside air hoods, power exhaust units, mixing boxes and economizers and energy recovery ventilators for many of the major HVAC manufactures.

In addition, MicroMetl provides customized products for unique applications. MicroMetl operates three manufacturing facilities that serve the HVAC industry from coast-to-coast. Our plants are located in Indianapolis, IN, Sparks, NV, and Longview, TX.
