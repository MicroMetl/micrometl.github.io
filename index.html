Curbs

Knocked Down, Welded and Vibration Isolation roof curbs constructed from galvanized sheet metal. Can be ordered with or without pitch and with or without insulation. Standard or Seismically Calculated to current IBC or CBC code requirements.

Economizers

Gear driven Vertical and Horizontal Economizers that are IECC, Title 24 and ASHRAE 90.1 code compliant. AMCA Class A rated dampers to meet the leakage criteria and use controls for Fault Detection and Diagnostic requirements.

Mixing Boxes

Gear driven Mixing Boxes that are IECC, Title 24 and ASHRAE 90.1 code compliant. AMCA Class A rated dampers to meet the leakage criteria and use controls for Fault Detection and Diagnostic requirements.

Power Exhaust

Vertical and Horizontal Power Exhaust to assist a rooftop package unit maintain static pressure within a given space. Centrifugal or Backward inclined blowers that can be 208/230V or 460V. Constant Volume or Modulating control options.
